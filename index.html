Mazda Online Earning Platform.

This is an online earning platform that provides tasks to freelancers. It provides trainings to freelancers on how to handle and present the tasks. It gives them the instructions on how to handle tasks and the appropriate way to get their payments on time. Below are some of the levels provided in this platform.

Basic level
The tasks are awarded to untrained freelancers.
Advanced level
The tasks are awarded to trained freelancers on basic jobs.
Premium level
The tasks are awarded to trained freelancers on advanced jobs.

Basic level.

The level requirement

  • Account sign up or registration.
  • Account activation fee of Kshs. 350 to be paid via mpesa.
  • A smartphone of version 12.0 and above.
  • Internet connection
  • Whatsapp number to be added on the members' group.
  • An upwork account for easy link-ups with the more online jobs.

Advanced level.

The level requirement

  • Account sign up or registration.
  • Account activation fee of Kshs. 550 to be paid via mpesa.
  • A laptop of atleast 4GB RAM and 500GB HDD or 256GB SSD or a smartphone.
  • Internet connection
  • Whatsapp number to be added on the members' group.
  • An upwork, Remotask accounts for easy link-ups with the more online jobs.

Premium level.

The level requirement

  • Account sign up or registration.
  • Account activation fee of Kshs. 550 to be paid via mpesa.
  • A laptop or Desktop of atleast 8GB RAM and 500GB HDD or 256GB SSD .
  • Internet connection
  • Whatsapp number to be added on the members' group.
  • An upwork, Remotask accounts for easy link-ups with the more online jobs.
